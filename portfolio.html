The Problem

A massive subscriber list sat dormant — sub-0.5% conversion rates. The brand was spending heavily to acquire traffic while failing entirely to retain it. Generic batch-and-blast emails had destroyed sender reputation and list trust.

The Fix

Migrated the full user base out of spray-and-pray newsletters into intent-based segmentation profiles. Rebuilt 4 core flows from scratch: Abandon Cart, Welcome Series, Post-Purchase Upsell, and Win-Back — each tuned by behavioral signal, not calendar date.

The Result

$90K+ in attributable email revenue recovered in under 90 days. CTR lifted 300%. Open rates stabilized. The list became a reliable, owned channel — not a graveyard.

Post-Mortem · Risk Ownership
Why the Initial Upsell Failed — and How We Fixed It

We drove immediate post-purchase upsells. Open rates hit 60%, but conversions were negligible — a mismatch between email intent (transactional receipts) and aggressive sales copy. Recognizing the problem fast, I delayed the upsell to the 3rd email in the sequence. That single timing shift secured trust and recovered 14% of second-purchase revenue immediately. Knowing when not to sell is the skill most marketers skip.
