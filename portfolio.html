Flagship Case Study

The Green Shades Black Friday Campaign

A visual discount system that worked across every channel — without copy or translation.

01 — Challenge
Black Friday is the noisiest sales window of the year

A funded trading platform needed a Black Friday concept that would cut through the noise across multiple channels simultaneously. Discount tiers had to be instantly understandable without relying on complex copy or language translations.

02 — Idea
Different shades of green = different discount intensities

Hamza proposed the "Green Shades" concept to his manager: use varying shades of green to represent discount tiers. The brighter the green, the bigger the discount. One visual system — universally readable, no translation needed. The manager approved the concept and provided strategic direction for the rollout.

03 — Execution
Coordinated rollout across 5 channels

Following the manager's strategic direction, Hamza owned the day-to-day execution: briefed the designer on the visual system, built email flows in Klaviyo, coordinated with the video editor and media buyer, and ensured consistent execution across the website, user dashboard, email, social media, and Discord. He managed the timeline and QA'd every touchpoint before launch.

04 — Result
$90,000+ in attributable revenue

The campaign contributed to $90,000+ in client revenue. Hamza received a 4,500 MAD performance bonus from the company in recognition of his contribution to the results.
